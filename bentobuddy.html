BentoBuddy

Helps commuting young adults plan, track, and maintain healthier eating routines around their busy schedules.

Duration: 3 months

Project Overview

BentoBuddy addresses the challenge of maintaining healthy eating habits for commuting workers in their 20s who struggle with time management. Through interviews with young adults, we found that long commutes and inconsistent schedules often lead to skipped meals and unhealthy food choices. BentoBuddy focuses on simplifying food scheduling by helping users log meals, set goals, and receive timely grocery and meal suggestions that fit into their daily routines.

Key Insights

✦ Young adults often skip breakfast due to their busy schedules

✦ Many young adults regularly eat out due to convenience

✦ People find it difficult to track their ethnic food intake and overall prefer a simpler method to track nutrition

Crowdy!

A real-time crowd monitoring app for navigating campus efficiently

Duration: 3 months

Project Overview

A mobile app that lets students check real-time crowd levels at campus locations like libraries, gyms, and dining halls to plan their visits efficiently. Built with my team, the app tracks foot traffic across popular spots and visualizes crowd levels through an intuitive, student-friendly interface. Crowdy makes it easier to plan your day, avoid long lines, and navigate campus more efficiently

My Role

Role: UX Researcher & Product Designer

Responsibilities: Usability testing, qualitative analysis, proposing design improvements

Platform: Mobile app

Our Goal

Students often arrive at campus locations only to find them overcrowded or unusable. Our goal was to evaluate whether a crowd-reporting app could help students make better, real-time decisions and to identify usability issues that might prevent students from actually using the app.

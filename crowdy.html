Crowdy!

A real-time crowd monitoring app for navigating campus efficiently

Duration: 3 months

Project Overview

The app visualizes foot traffic levels so users can avoid long lines and plan their day better. Built with my team, the app tracks foot traffic across popular spots and visualizes crowd levels through an intuitive, student-friendly interface. Crowdy makes it easier to plan your day, avoid long lines, and navigate campus more efficiently

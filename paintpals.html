Project Overview

PaintPals is a collaborative drawing app designed to make creating art more social and playful. Built during a 3-day designathon, the app allows users to draw individually, share artwork with friends, and collaborate through a timed, turn-based game mode that results in a single shared drawing.

The Problem

Drawing is often a solo activity, and many creative apps focus on individual creation rather than shared experiences. We wanted to explore how collaboration and light gamification could make art more social and engaging.
